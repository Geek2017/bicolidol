ABOUT

89.1 Bicol Idol FM is the hottest radio station on the rise at live broadcast on our dial at 89.1 FM in Legazpi City, Albay. We pride ourselves and please our valued listeners with programs on latest news, unbiased public affairs program and queuing wide variety of music both old and new. BEST VARIETY, COOLEST HITS. 89.1 Bicol Idol FM offers a space for to enjoy new and all-time favorite music.

BACKGROUND

Collective in-depth brainstorming sessions gave birth to 89.1 BICOL IDOL FM which is envisioned to be an epitome of a pro-active and socially responsible broadcast journalism outfit and setting a strategic direction of its integrative broadcasting activities geared towards an outcome-based and balance program format. Likewise, 89.1 IDOL FM aims to be the flagship in helping local business entrepreneurs through timely updates on the latest business trends and provide an avenue for them to listen, earn and invest.

89.1 BICOL IDOL FM

89.1 BICOL IDOL FM is committed to achieve transformative goals based on quality standard system of operation through innovative, pro-active, informative and environment friendly approaches. It aims at delivering timely and balanced news and information, relevant agri-business trends, wholesome entertainment, public service and outreach programs. Broadcast operations are well within the framework and guiding principles of the KBP Standards Authority.
